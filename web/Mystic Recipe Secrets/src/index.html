The Art of

Spellbinding

Cookie Creation

Enchanting Ingredients

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted under the moonlight.
  2. ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, harvested from the Dark Forest.
  3. 1 ½ tsp baking soda, ground from enchanted stones.
  4. ½ tsp salt, gathered from the Sea of Dreams.
  5. 1 cup sugar, spun by the Wind Spirits.
  6. 1 egg, laid by the Phoenix.
  7. 1 cup whole milk, freshly enchanted by woodland creatures.
  8. ½ cup unsalted butter, softened under a gentle fire spell.
  9. 1 tsp vanilla extract, brewed by ancient herbalists.
  10. For the filling: 1 cup marshmallow fluff, enchanted from the clouds, and 1 cup butter, whipped to perfection.

The Ritual

  1. Preheat your cauldron (or oven) to 350°F.
  2. In a large enchanted bowl, combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Stir while chanting the incantation of balance.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar in a separate bowl until light and fluffy, blending with the magic of patience.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the creamed mixture, whispering the spell of unity.
  5. Alternate adding the flour mixture and milk, ensuring they merge into a smooth, magical batter.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of batter onto a parchment-lined baking sheet, casting a spell of even distribution.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, watching as the pies rise with a soft, airy texture. Cool completely under the protective aura of the cooling rack.
  8. For the filling, blend the butter and marshmallow fluff until they become a velvety potion, perfect for bringing the pies to life.
  9. Spread the filling onto the flat side of one pie and sandwich with another, sealing the magic within.
  10. Let the Whoopie Pies rest for a few minutes before enjoying the spellbinding creation.
