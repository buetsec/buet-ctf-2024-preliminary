Red Velvet Cupcakes

Mystic Ingredients

  1. 2 large dragon eggs (room temperature)
  2. 1 cup crimson sugar (granulated)
  3. 1 stick butter (½ cup), softened by fire sprites
  4. 1 teaspoon of enchanted vanilla extract
  5. 1 ½ cups all-purpose spellbound flour
  6. 1 ½ teaspoons of ancient baking powder
  7. 1 pinch of salt from the Eternal Sea
  8. ½ cup moon milk
  9. 1 tablespoon of red potion (food coloring)
  10. 1 teaspoon cocoa powder (dark magic dust)
  11. 1 teaspoon white vinegar (balancer of spells)

The Ritual

  1. Preheat your magical oven to 350°F and line your enchanted cupcake molds with cupcake liners to contain the magical essence.
  2. In a large bowl, summon together the dragon eggs and crimson sugar. Add the softened butter and enchanted vanilla extract, and mix with a mystic whisk until smooth and creamy.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift the flour, baking powder, salt, and dark magic dust (cocoa powder). Whisper the incantation of balance as you prepare these dry ingredients.
  4. In three stages, carefully add the dry ingredients to the egg-and-sugar mixture, alternating with the moon milk. Stir with care, but avoid overmixing to preserve the magic.
  5. Add the red potion (food coloring), and finish with a dash of vinegar to activate the magical reaction, giving the cupcakes their signature red hue and spellbinding rise.
  6. Scoop the enchanted batter into your prepared cupcake molds, but be cautious not to overfill them, for they will rise under the oven's magic. Bake for 17-20 minutes until the cakes are firm but springy.
  7. Remove your Red Velvet Cupcakes from the oven, letting them cool on an enchanted rack for 2 minutes before fully resting.
