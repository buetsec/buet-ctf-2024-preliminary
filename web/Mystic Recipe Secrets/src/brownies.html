Making

Strawberry Brownies

Mystic Ingredients

  1. 1 cup butter, melted under moonlight
  2. 2 cups enchanted white sugar
  3. ½ cup cocoa powder, infused with strawberry essence
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, whispered with charm
  5. 4 dragon eggs (or regular eggs)
  6. 1 ½ cups all-purpose fairy flour
  7. ½ teaspoon of magical baking powder
  8. ½ teaspoon salt from the Eternal Sea
  9. 1 cup fresh strawberry slices (or dried strawberry magic dust)

The Ritual

  1. Preheat the enchanted oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan, casting a protective spell on it.
  2. In a large spellbound bowl, combine the melted moonlit butter, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, and dragon eggs. Mix gently, then add in the fairy flour, magical baking powder, and Eternal Sea salt.
  3. Once the batter is prepared, fold in the strawberry slices, infusing them with your own wishes for sweetness.
  4. Spread the batter evenly into your prepared pan, murmuring the final incantation for perfection.
  5. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes, or until your Strawberry Brownies pass the toothpick test, ensuring the magic has worked its way through.
  6. Cool on a wire rack, letting the brownies rest and absorb the spell of deliciousness.
